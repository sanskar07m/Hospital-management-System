About MediCare Hospital

Our Story

MediCare Hospital was established in 1985 with a vision to provide exceptional healthcare services to our community. For over 35 years, we have been dedicated to delivering quality medical care with compassion and integrity.

What began as a small clinic has now grown into a state-of-the-art medical facility equipped with the latest technology and staffed by experienced healthcare professionals. Our commitment to patient-centered care has remained unwavering throughout our journey.

Today, MediCare Hospital stands as a beacon of hope and healing, serving thousands of patients each year with a comprehensive range of medical services.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality healthcare services that promote the well-being of our patients and community through compassionate care, innovative treatments, and continuous improvement.

Our Vision

To be the region's premier healthcare provider, recognized for excellence in patient care, medical innovation, and community health advancement.

Our Values

  • Compassion: Treating patients with kindness and empathy
  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in healthcare
  • Integrity: Maintaining honesty and ethical conduct
  • Teamwork: Collaborating for better patient outcomes
  • Innovation: Embracing new ideas and technologies
