Frequently Asked Questions

What are your visiting hours?

General visiting hours are from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM daily. Different departments may have specific visiting policies, so we recommend checking with the relevant ward or unit.

How can I access my medical records?

You can request your medical records by submitting a written request to our Medical Records Department. You can also call (123) 456-7893 or email records@medicare-hospital.com for assistance.

What insurance plans do you accept?

We accept most major insurance plans. Please contact our billing department at (123) 456-7894 to verify if your specific insurance is accepted.

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment by calling (123) 456-7891, visiting our appointment page, or by visiting our hospital in person.
