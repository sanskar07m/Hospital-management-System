Our Medical Specialties

Comprehensive Medical Care

At MediCare Hospital, we offer a wide range of specialized medical services to address various health concerns. Our team of experienced doctors, advanced technology, and compassionate care ensure that you receive the best treatment possible for your specific needs.

Cardiology

Our cardiology department provides comprehensive diagnosis and treatment for heart-related conditions. Our cardiologists use advanced technology to perform detailed heart assessments, including ECGs, stress tests, and cardiac imaging.

Key Services:

  • Cardiac consultations and check-ups
  • Heart disease management
  • Cardiac rehabilitation programs
  • Preventive cardiology

Neurology

Our neurology department specializes in diagnosing and treating disorders of the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, and nerves. Our neurologists are experts in managing conditions such as stroke, epilepsy, headaches, and neurodegenerative disorders.

Key Services:

  • Neurological evaluations
  • EEG and EMG testing
  • Stroke management
  • Treatment of neurodegenerative diseases

Orthopedics

Our orthopedic specialists focus on conditions involving the musculoskeletal system. We provide comprehensive care for bone, joint, and muscle disorders, whether they are the result of injury, aging, or disease.

Key Services:

  • Joint replacement surgery
  • Fracture care
  • Sports medicine
  • Physical therapy and rehabilitation

Pediatrics

Our pediatric department is dedicated to the health and well-being of children from birth through adolescence. Our child-friendly environment and specialized pediatricians ensure that younger patients receive appropriate care for their unique needs.

Key Services:

  • Well-child visits and vaccinations
  • Developmental assessments
  • Pediatric consultations
  • Management of childhood illnesses

Gynecology

Our gynecology department offers comprehensive women's health services. Our specialists focus on the female reproductive system and provide care for women at all stages of life, from adolescence through menopause and beyond.

Key Services:

  • Routine gynecological exams
  • Family planning services
  • Prenatal care
  • Treatment of reproductive disorders

Dermatology

Our dermatology department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of skin, hair, and nail conditions. From common skin problems to complex dermatological disorders, our dermatologists provide expert care using the latest treatments.

Key Services:

  • Skin cancer screening and treatment
  • Acne and eczema management
  • Laser treatments
  • Cosmetic dermatology procedures

Need Specialized Medical Care?

Our expert team is ready to provide you with the best treatment for your specific health concerns.
